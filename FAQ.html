FAQs

Facts and Questions

01
"What is your trade credit?"

We offer a 2/10 net 30, which means if you pay withing 10 days, you recieve a 2% discount. Otherwise the full bill is due after 30 days.

02
"Can I get this job as soon as possible?"

If a job is needed as soon as possible we can give it priority over other in progress jobs and or even work on it outside of regular business hours albeit at a surcharge for inconvenience. The best way to get a job back as soon as possible however will always be getting it to us as soon as possible.

03
"Is there a credit payment option?"

We offer a credit payment option upon request. Reach out and specify when initially placing the order to recieve further information so that we may help setup a payment plan.
