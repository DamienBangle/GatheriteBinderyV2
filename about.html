We Provide Professional Bindery Services

Gatherite Bindery was originally founded by the Reed family in 1987 in the back of the family's small laundromat located in downtown East Dundee as a father and son business venture. Originally starting off with just a couple employees and a few pieces of used equipment, Gatherite bindery slowly grew from this humble beginning to finding itself doing finishing work for some of the largest printers in the midwest like R.R. Donnelley, Bell Litho, and the Schiele Group. Gatherite Bindery now boasts decades of experience in nearly every facet in the bindery industry.
